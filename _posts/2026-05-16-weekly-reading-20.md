[LLM-based Hospital Course Summaries and Physician Burnout](https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jamanetworkopen/fullarticle/2848785?utm_campaign=articlePDF&utm_medium=articlePDFlink&utm_source=articlePDF&utm_content=jamanetworkopen.2026.16556)

> In this study, an LLM-based agentic workflow produced hospital course summaries that were frequently used with minimal risk of harm identified. The intervention was associated with a reduction in physician burnout, supporting the viability of AI summarization to mitigate documentation burden.
